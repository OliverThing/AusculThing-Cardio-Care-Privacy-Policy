AusculThing ACC Software Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13th of October 2023

At AusculThing Ltd., we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your data when you use the AusculThing Cardio Care (ACC) software.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: This includes your email address used for sending results and any other personal details you provide within the app.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with our software, such as features used and time spent.
  • Device Data: Information about your device, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our software.
  • To notify you about changes to our software.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To gather analysis or valuable information to improve our software.
  • To monitor the usage of our software.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

3. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will also retain usage data for internal analysis purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data as well as your account. You can send us an email at info@ausculthing.com for any data-related requests.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date at the top.

6. To Delete Your Account

Your data may be deleted from our system by sending a request to info@ausculthing.com.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: info@ausculthing.com
  • By visiting our website: www.ausculthing.com
